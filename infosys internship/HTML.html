We will share with you a Task Submission form through which you can submit your tasks. If you have not received the form in the onboarding email, please continue working on your tasks. What is - LinkedIn Profile Improvement?

(Linkedin Profile Improvement) aime to give you a strong professional public profile on Linkedin and help you with your job hunt. A strong profile gets the attention of recruiters more frequently. To complete this task, please follow these steps:

1. Read some online articles to understand how you can make your Linkedin profile as awesome one.

2.Add Intern at Oasis Infobyte as your Linkedin profile title and add it as your current work experience as well.


3. Share all your achievements which you have got from us (Offer Letter, Completion Certificate, LOR) also tag @oasis infobyte and use #oasisinfobyte in such posts.
